ALHASSAN DANTATA

A Successful Business Man

  • BORN- 1877 in Bebeji, Kano State, Nigeria.

  • CAREER- He was a successful long-distance trader that trade, produce and export groundnuts, Kolanut, cattle, beads, precious stones, grains , rope and other commodities.
    He was also the first to develop the trade route to transport goods between Accra, Kumasi, Sekondi and Lagos. This route connected him with the European traders which further helped his wealth expand.

  • DESCENDANTS-
    Sanusi Dantata (1917-1997): son, a successful businessman
    Alhaji Mudi Dantata(1918-2005), son, a successful Businessman
    Ahmadu Dantata (1916-1960): son, a politician.
    Aminu Dantata (1931- ): son, a businessman.

  • DEATH- Died in his sleep on Wednesday 17 August 1955.
